Who I Am

My name is Tariq. I currently work in New York City as a Senior Software Engineer in Test at Twitter. Previously I was at Turbonomic where I started as a Software Engineer in Test and then I was promoted to manage the same team. I helped the company to build a test automation platform that automates testing for the backend, frontend and APIs of the company’s software. The company provides a workload automation platform that makes it easier for customers to automatically control and manage their IT virtualized environment. Before that I was a Software Developer at ProgressSoft. I was a member of a team that developed the world’s first Electronic Cheque Clearing system called PS-ECC. I worked on improving the company’s products in addition to developing integration systems between the company’s products and the banking systems.
